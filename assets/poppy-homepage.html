Why I built this AI tool
I have ADHD and I am neurodivergent (aka neuro spicy haha). I go down rabbit holes, research things forever, and often tend to procrastinate as soon as it's time to turn my idea into action. (let's be real the idea part of it is more fun anyway )
I've been using ChatGPT and Claude since literally the minute they came out and find it insane that majority of the people still don't know or don't use AI to help them in their day to day. Just walking around being 20 IQ points lower for no absolute reason is diabolical work in my opinion.
But I hate the fact that all AI tools are linear as in they go from top to bottom. Like a book. I've always felt so constrained using them like I'm in a strait jacket.
I wanted an experience that felt more like a choose your own adventure video game. An experience where I felt free in the way I could speak to the AI to have it work with me. I could talk to it through voice or text or an image or even a video. Somewhere I could visually stitch together all my thoughts and have AI make meaning out of it.
I wanted to be able to SEE all the knowledge it had at any given point. Something that didn't hallucinate or forget my conversation every few minutes.
Another problem I had was as a content creator and a copywriter whenever I had to write something I HATED sitting there looking at an empty blinking cursor on GOOGLE DOCS or constantly switching between chrome tabs to look at ChatGPT (uninspiring linear text blob) back to Google Docs (another uninspiring linear text blob).
I wanted to be able to throw all my research together and then have the AI write for me in my own voice. What I hated about regular ChatGPT was that I couldn't do that. It would always write me something cringy in a voice that never feels like my own.
I built this because my brain thinks non-linearly. My thoughts are a chaotic mess. I need something to slobber my thoughts all over.
Then use AI to stitch it together and make meaning out of it.
And ultimately to produce real world published work like emails that get 100k+ opens and videos that get millions of views. Doing it in a way that saves me 100's of hours and leaves me feeling inspired for doing more work.
